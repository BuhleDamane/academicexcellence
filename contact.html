Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you review my assignment?

Our standard turnaround time is 24-48 hours for most assignments. For urgent requests, we offer same-day service with an additional rush fee. The exact timeframe depends on the length and complexity of your work.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EFT, credit cards, and mobile payments. A consultation fee and deposit are required upfront, with the balance due upon completion of your project.

Do you provide revision services?

Yes, we offer one free revision within 7 days of delivery if the work doesn't meet the original specifications. Additional revisions may incur extra charges.

Is my work kept confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality for all client work. Your documents and personal information are never shared with third parties and are securely stored.
